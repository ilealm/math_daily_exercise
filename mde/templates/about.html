The purpose of this website

As a dyslexic myself, I remember how I struggled to learn the product tables.

I wanted to create a website where kids (and adults) can safely practice the product tables and get a progress record, while helping parents to monitor their kid's daily math practice without overwhelming them and affecting the parent-child relationship.
