The purpose of this website

As a dyslexic myself, I remember how I struggled to learn the product tables, so I wanted to create a way where kids (and adults) can safely practice them and get a progress record.

Also, I wanted to help parents to monitor their kid's daily math practice without overwhelming them and affecting the parent-child relationship.
